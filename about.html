About

Past

My past is full of learning experiences from all over the country. I was originally from Michigan but I moved to California as a child. I spent a lot of my childhood playing sports and going to auditions trying to be an actor. But, after fifth grade I moved back to Michigan to be closer to family. During my schooling, I went a different route. I chose to go into robotics, engineering, and automotive. So, once high school rolled around, I was enrolled in a technical campus as well as a regular high school. I went to the technical campus for engineering and automotive while being on the robotics club. After high school, I was introduced to even more experiences by getting accepted into Michigan State University.

Present

During my time at Michigan State University (MSU), I was introduced to the world of advertising. Doing culinary on the side, I learned many computer programs necessary to thrive on the creative side of advertising. My eyes were opened up to just how much work is needed to make campaigns happen. People spend mostly everyday exposed to advertising of some sort but those moments when you get someone to actually stop and look at your work just can't be described. So, as of now, I’m focused on practicing my skills. Trying to get on that level of creativity where everything I produce will catch the consumer’s eyes and create a lasting memory. Through the use of the Adobe creative suite and my ability to generate ideas on the spot, the future looks bright.

Future

As for my future, I want to work with a company that's going places. Not saying that I haven't worked for established and important companies, because I've worked in the culinary industry for Michigan State as well as worked in Florida at Walt Disney World. But, what I’m looking for is a company that really understands what the consumer wants and not just trying to get by. A company that's looking for someone like me that wants to see something made that gets the recognition it deserves. Also, a company that's fun but at the same time likes to take risks because that's where true innovation comes from.
